Who made Transformers?

Transformers was created by Hasbro and Takara, inspired by Japanese robot toys. Marvel Comics built the characters and storyline to help sell the toys, leading to the iconic Autobots vs Decepticons universe.

Who shapes Transformers?

Michael Bay elevated Transformers into a global blockbuster franchise with his five live-action films, introducing a realistic, military-driven style and massive visual effects that brought mainstream success.
